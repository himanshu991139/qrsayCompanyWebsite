What's QR Code & How it Works?

 

A quick response code is a machine-readable optical label that can contain information about the item to which it is attached. A QR code consists of black squares arranged in a square grid on a white background, which can be read by an imaging device such as a camera. QR codes have become common in consumer advertising. A QR Code works by simply scanning the code with a mobile device that is equipped with a camera and QR Code reader application. These applications can be downloaded for free on popular smartphone platforms such as iPhone and Android.

Once the QR Code is scanned using the device’s camera it is translated into actionable information, such as a text message or mobile web page. Benefits of QR Code for Businesses

 

Many business get lot of benefits from QR code food ordering system.

1. CINEMA

QR food ordering menu code helps to stay updated and keeps the audience engaged. In last few years there has been access to smartphone and high speed internet. We can see QR code food ordering in cinemas. Once you scan the code you can get full information about the food & beverage menu you placed order for an interval. By scanning the process becomes faster and smoother. It can also be used at the entry gate to grant entry into theater. Also video QR code helps to promote the trailer of any new movie which is yet to be released. Just scanning the code the person comes to know about all information about the food & beverage menu and places the order via the mobile without downloading any app.

Contactless cinema food ordering system is possible through QR code in theatres. People just scan and order their refreshments during interval. There is less of human interaction i.e. staff contact with customers and this safeguards their health. Digital menus can replace paper menus and this reduces the cost of printing.

2. RESTAURANTS

One can access digital menus in restaurants through QR code food ordering system. It gives flexibility in menu updation or menu changes. Restaurants can quickly change new menus and add descriptions, pictures to the item. It reduces time of printing a menu. Customers can get contactless options. Even digital payments are so popular that just using QR code one can pay faster. The restaurants can train their staff to become more hospitalized and can be more customer-centric.

In restaurants, QR food order system can lead your customers easily scan the digital food menu on their mobile via QR codes without downloading any application. The QR code food ordering in restaurants enables a seamless customer experience across all ordering channels. Restaurants can gain a profit by selling food in advance. Customers can profit from not wasting time as well. It’s a win-win for both restaurants and customers.

3. HOTELS

Hotels can also get lot of benefits from QR food ordering system. As its cost effective and flexible. Hotels can promote their property through QR Code easily and it can reach large number of people. Customers get contact free check-in, fast as we all know hygiene is the new security. Hotels gave faster access to room locations, menu and other options. As digitize do not disturb so hotels can become more efficient in their operations and concentrate on customer service and promotion of their place.

Using our contactless QR code food ordering in hotels, your guests scan the QR code, view the room service menu and place the order from their mobile phone browser without downloading any application. So, the guest won’t have to wait for waiters. The hotel ordering system can increase sales, decrease your costs, speed-up room service.

4. CORPORATES

A QR code is present everywhere and everyplace. Even in corporates. QR code has become important communication tool as it increases brand awareness, proximity to customers and also facilitates financial control. In business QR code can be very useful, as this code if scanned can open forms/web pages/digital payment page in jiffy.
