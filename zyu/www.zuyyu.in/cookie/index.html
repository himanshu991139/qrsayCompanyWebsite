1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Zuyyu Private Limited ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website or use our software or application (collectively, "Services"). This policy provides information about what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners. Cookies can be categorized as:

Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that are deleted from your device when you close your web browser.

Persistent Cookies: Cookies that remain on your device until they expire or are deleted by you.

First-party Cookies: Set by the website you are visiting.

Third-party Cookies: Set by a domain other than the one you are visiting.

3. How We Use Cookies
3.1 Essential Cookies:

These cookies are necessary for the basic functionality of our website and software. They enable you to navigate our platform, use essential features, and ensure the security of your data.

3.2 Analytics Cookies:

Analytics cookies help us collect and analyze information about how our website and software are used. They provide valuable insights into user behavior, such as the pages you visit, the duration of your visits, and any errors you encounter. This information is used to improve our services and enhance your user experience.

3.3 Advertising and Marketing Cookies:

TAdvertising and marketing cookies are used to deliver personalized advertisements and marketing content. These cookies track your online activity to tailor advertising based on your interests and browsing behavior.

3.4 Functionality Cookies:

Functionality cookies enhance your user experience by remembering your preferences and settings. They ensure that you have a more personalized and convenient experience during your visits.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some of the cookies on our website and within our software may be set by third parties. These cookies help us integrate third-party content or services, such as analytics tools or social media features. We do not have control over these cookies. Please review the respective third-party's privacy and cookie policies for more information.

    5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

    You have the option to manage your cookie preferences. Most web browsers allow you to control cookie settings, including blocking or deleting cookies. However, please be aware that disabling certain cookies may impact the functionality of our website and software.

    6. Updates to this Cookie Policy

    We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes to our cookie practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The updated policy will be posted on our website, and the date of the latest revision will be indicated.

    7. Contact Us

    If you have any questions about our use of cookies or this Cookie Policy, please contact us at sales@zuyyu.in.
